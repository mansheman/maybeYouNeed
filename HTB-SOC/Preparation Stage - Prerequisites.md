# Preparation Stage - Prerequisites
[[Tags/SOC]] [[Tags/HTB]] [[Tags/IncidentHandling]] [[Tags/Preparation]]

The Preparation stage has two main objectives:
1. Establishing incident handling capability.
2. Implementing protective measures to prevent incidents (hardening, MFA, AD tiering).

### Prerequisites for Preparation
![[Pics/HTB-SOC/ir_preparation.png]]

- **Skilled Team**: Internal capability is essential even if specific tasks are outsourced.
- **Trained Workforce**: Security awareness training.
- **Clear Policies & Documentation**: Defined roles and procedures.
- **Tools**: Both hardware and software ready for immediate use.
