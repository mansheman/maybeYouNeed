# Incident Reporting
[[Tags/SOC]] [[Tags/HTB]] [[Tags/Reporting]]

Documentation of security incidents should align with frameworks like **Cyber Kill Chain** and **MITRE ATT&CK**.

### Incident-Specific Reports
Focus on one particular event or outbreak, walking step-by-step through the attack lifecycle.
- **Example:** [The DFIR Report](https://thedfirreport.com/)
![[Pics/HTB-SOC/dfirreport.png]]

### Global Incident-Response Reports
Aggregate data from hundreds of incidents to identify trends and statistical insights.
- **Example:** [Palo Alto Unit 42 Global Incident Response Report](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/engage/unit42-2025-global-incident-response-report)
- **Trend (2024):** 86% of incidents involved business disruption (downtime or reputational damage).
