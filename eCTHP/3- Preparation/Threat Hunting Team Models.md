# Threat Hunting Team Models

There is no single standard threat hunting team model.

The structure depends on:

- organization size
- industry
- network complexity
- available staff
- threat exposure

## Common models

Ad-hoc hunter:

- multiple IT or security responsibilities
- hunts are task-oriented
- common in smaller organizations

Analyst and hunter:

- a SOC analyst also performs hunts
- common in medium-sized organizations
- hunting is folded into daily operations

Dedicated hunting team:

- hunting is the sole responsibility of the team
- deeper expertise
- more structured and recurring hunts
