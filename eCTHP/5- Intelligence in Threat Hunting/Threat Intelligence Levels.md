# Threat Intelligence Levels

Each intelligence level answers a different question for a different audience.

## Strategic

- focus: vision, priorities, long-term risk
- audience: executives and program owners

## Operational

- focus: campaign context and adversary activity patterns
- audience: CTI leads, IR leads, hunt leads

## Tactical

- focus: defensive behavior and what teams should detect
- audience: SOC, hunters, detection engineers

## Technical

- focus: indicators and artifacts that can be searched or blocked
- audience: analysts and security tools

## Key takeaway

Best results come from connecting all four levels and keeping the feedback loop active.
