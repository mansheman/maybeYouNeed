# Threat Intelligence Lifecycle and Prioritization

## CTI lifecycle

The CTI lifecycle is iterative.

1. planning and direction
2. collection
3. processing and exploitation
4. analysis and production
5. dissemination
6. feedback

The goal is actionable intelligence, not just raw collection.

## Prioritization

Threat teams cannot act on every feed item or report.

Useful prioritization asks:

- is this relevant to our environment?
- is it actionable?
- what is the likely impact if it is real?

## Common scoring approaches

- Admiralty Code
- Diamond Model
- risk-based scoring such as likelihood x impact x exposure
