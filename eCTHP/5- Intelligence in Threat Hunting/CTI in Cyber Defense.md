# CTI in Cyber Defense

Threat intelligence turns isolated observations into context defenders can act on.

## What CTI adds

- indicators of compromise
- TTPs
- attribution clues
- context such as sector targeting, timing, and victimology

## Where it supports defense

- detection through IOC matching and alert enrichment
- response through playbooks and contextual triage
- prevention through prioritization and hardening
- recovery through lessons learned
- planning through risk assessments and exercises

## Integration points

Useful CTI should feed into:

- SIEM
- EDR
- firewall or WAF controls
- SOAR workflows
- threat-intelligence platforms
