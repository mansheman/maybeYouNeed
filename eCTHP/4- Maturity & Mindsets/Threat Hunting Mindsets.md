# Threat Hunting Mindsets

Effective hunting depends on a mindset that is:

- skeptical
- evidence-driven
- hypothesis-oriented
- willing to challenge assumptions

## Important habits

- avoid confirmation bias
- test assumptions against telemetry
- look for disconfirming evidence
- think in attacker objectives, not only indicators
