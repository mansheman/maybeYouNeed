# Threats, APTs, and TTPs

## Threat

A threat is any potential negative impact or danger to an asset.

## APT

APT stands for Advanced Persistent Threat.

Common characteristics:

- long-term detection evasion
- multi-stage attacks
- quick use of zero-days
- custom exploitation and tooling

## TTPs

TTPs stands for Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures.

- tactics describe attacker goals
- techniques describe how a tactic is implemented
- procedures describe the exact operational steps

TTPs matter because they describe attacker behavior, not just tools.
