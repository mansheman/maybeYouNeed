# Kill Chain and ATT&CK

## Cyber Kill Chain

The Cyber Kill Chain is a staged model that describes attacker activity from early discovery to the final objective.

Stages:

- reconnaissance
- weaponization
- delivery
- exploitation
- installation
- command and control
- actions on objectives

## MITRE ATT&CK

MITRE ATT&CK is a knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques based on real-world observations.

Common uses:

- mapping observed TTPs
- comparing overlaps with tracked groups
- identifying detection gaps
- building hunt hypotheses
