# Threat Actors and Delivery

## Attacker types and motivations

Threat actors are often easiest to reason about by starting with access:

- external attackers begin without authorized access
- internal attackers already have some level of access

Common types:

- nation-state actors
- advanced persistent threats
- organized crime
- hacktivists
- insider threats

## Threat vectors and malware delivery

Common vectors:

- removable storage
- malicious or weaponized files
- phishing and messaging delivery
- vulnerabilities and outdated systems
- open ports and exposed services
- supply-chain compromise

## Hunting implication

The vector explains how the threat entered.

The malware explains what the attacker can do next.

Telemetry shows where you should look.
