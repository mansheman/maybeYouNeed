# Hunt Goals and Dwell Time

## Primary goals

- detect advanced threats in infrastructure
- identify threats routine monitoring may miss
- reduce the amount of time attackers remain in the environment
- find attacks in earlier stages before major damage occurs

## Dwell time

Dwell time is the amount of time an adversary remains on the network undetected while retaining access.

During this period, an attacker may:

- gather and exfiltrate data
- collect credentials
- move laterally
- escalate privileges
- deploy persistence or destructive payloads

## Why it matters

The longer dwell time is, the more opportunity the attacker has to:

- expand access
- steal data
- damage systems
- establish durable persistence

Threat hunting aims to shorten dwell time by identifying threats earlier.
